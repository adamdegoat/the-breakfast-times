On This Day

1980

On this day in 1980, the world's first round-the-clock news channel switched on — the then-strange idea that you could find out what was happening anywhere, at any hour. A small marvel worth remembering: most days, somewhere, someone is choosing to keep the lights on so the rest of us stay connected.
