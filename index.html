Habit of the day

You wake up mildly dehydrated after a night's sleep — and that's half of the groggy, foggy feeling most of us blame on not being a morning person. A single glass of water before the coffee rehydrates you, softens the caffeine jitters, and gently wakes your digestion. It costs nothing and takes ten seconds, and most people who try it for a week quietly never stop.
